Sample Employee Guidelines
Attendance- Working hours: 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM.- Mark attendance before 9:15 AM.- Inform your manager if you will be late.
Work From Home- Ensure a stable internet connection.- Be available during core hours (10 AM - 4 PM).- Attend all scheduled meetings.- Maintain a distraction-free workspace.
Leave Policy- Apply for leave through the HR portal in advance.- Emergency leave should be communicated immediately.
Meeting Etiquette- Join meetings on time.- Mute yourself when not speaking.- Keep camera on when requested.
Security- Do not share passwords.- Lock your device when away.- Report suspicious emails to IT.
Code of Conduct- Treat everyone with respect.- Follow company policies.- Maintain confidentiality of company information
